Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup (about 100g) Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled (optional)
  • Black pepper, freshly ground
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, saut√© the pancetta or guanciale until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper.
  4. When the pasta is cooked, reserve a cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti and add it to the skillet with the pancetta. Toss to combine.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing continuously to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water if needed.
  6. Serve immediately with an extra sprinkle of Pecorino Romano and black pepper.
